## üìß Email Customization Notes

### For YC Companies:
- Mention batch (S24, W25, etc.)
- Reference "fellow YC founder" vibe
- Keep it short (they get 100+ emails/day)

### For Well-Funded Startups:
- Lead with recent funding news
- Mention specific product features
- ROI angle (save eng time)

### For Consultancies:
- Partnership model (50/50 split)
- White-label opportunity
- Reference similar client success

### For VCs:
- Ask for portfolio intros
- Offer to present at portfolio event
- Keep it super brief (2-3 sentences)

### For Indie Hackers:
- Peer-to-peer tone
- Offer free implementation help
- Build relationship, not immediate sale
