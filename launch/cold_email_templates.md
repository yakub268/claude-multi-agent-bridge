**Signals They Need This:**
- Job postings for "AI Engineer" or "ML Infrastructure"
- Blog posts about AI agents, LLMs, automation

- YC company directory (filter for AI)
- LinkedIn: Search "building with Claude" or "AI agents"

- Show HN: Companies that posted about AI products

**How Many to Send:**
- Week 1: 10 emails (test messaging)
- Week 2: 25 emails (if 10% response rate)
- Week 3: 50 emails (if conversion is working)

**Expected Metrics:**
- Open rate: 40-60% (personalized)
- Response rate: 10-20%
- Call booking rate: 5-10%
- Close rate: 20-30% of calls

**Math:**
- Send 50 emails
- Get 7 calls
- Close 2 projects at $5k-8k each
- Revenue: $10k-16k

## Email Best Practices

**✅ Do:**
- Personalize first line (mention their product/blog/hiring)
- Lead with value (what you built, metrics)
- One clear CTA (book a call)
- Keep under 150 words
- Include GitHub link (social proof)

**❌ Don't:**
- Generic "I saw your company" openings
- Long backstory about yourself
- Multiple CTAs (confusing)
- Sell before showing value
- Forget to follow up (80% of sales happen after 5+ touchpoints)
